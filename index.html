How this is calculated —
Salary compounds yearly at your growth rate. Each year's PVD contribution = salary × (employee % + employer %), added to a non-invested PVD balance. From the investment start age onward, that same year's PVD contribution is also routed into an investment account that compounds annually at your chosen return rate.
